## 🎓 Infinity Medical Academy

**Infinity Medical Academy**, accredited by the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties, is a beacon of innovative training for healthcare students and professionals. This project encapsulates a cutting-edge platform, ingeniously crafted to elevate the educational experience in the healthcare domain.

### My Role and Contributions

- **Front-End Development:** Collaboratively spearheaded the front-end development, focusing on forging a website that's not only user-friendly but also engaging, delivering a seamless and interactive user experience.
- **Agile Methodology:** Actively engaged in Agile methodologies within a professional team setting, playing a key role in ensuring efficient and timely project execution.

### Key Project Features

- **Comprehensive Administrators and Trainers Dashboard:** A sophisticated tool for streamlined course and user management.
- **Intuitive Customer Web Application:** A user-centric interface, tailored for ease of use by learners and instructors.
- **Automated Certificate Generation:** Enhancing the efficiency of the certification process.
- **Payment Gateway and Zoom Integration:** Facilitates secure transactions and interactive virtual learning sessions.
- **Dynamic Pricing and Discounts:** Offering flexible payment solutions to cater to a diverse range of clients.

### Use Cases

- Empowering healthcare professionals and students with top-tier training and certification.
- Streamlining administrative processes in educational course management.
- Providing a robust, user-friendly platform for healthcare education.
